# Phase 1A: AI Datacenter/Infrastructure Comparables Research

**Prepared for:** Gravitas Valuation Intelligence Platform
**Date:** 2026-05-21
**Scope:** AI datacenter/infrastructure comparables for Alpine AI Infrastructure AG (Gravitas)
**Research Agent:** DEE-17

---

## Executive Summary

This research brief covers 11 AI datacenter/infrastructure comparables spanning Swiss, European, and global markets. Comparables were selected across 8 categories: AI-native datacenter startups, GPU infrastructure providers, HPC infrastructure, sovereign cloud, AI colocation, green datacenter operators, renewable-powered AI infrastructure, and modular datacenter platforms.

**Gravitas reference parameters:** CHF 0.10/kWh contracted power, 20-year PPA, PUE 1.08 target, 25MW Phase 1 (55MW full entitlement), behind-the-meter structure, sovereign-grade counterparties.

## 6. Data Gaps and Limitations

| Gap | Impact | Suggested Alternative |
|---|---|---|
| PitchBook, CB Insights, S&P Capital IQ — paywalled | Cannot access detailed funding history, cap tables, or private valuations for pre-IPO companies | **Source unavailable** — used public Crunchbase, press releases, SEC filings as fallback |
| Preqin — paywalled | Cannot access infrastructure fund return data or detailed transaction multiples | **Source unavailable** — used public transaction announcements |
| Energy cost per kWh for most comparables | Cannot directly benchmark against Gravitas CHF 0.10/kWh | Norwegian comparables likely €0.02-0.04/kWh; Swiss comparables ~CHF 0.08-0.15/kWh based on market data |
| PUE data sparse | Only 3 of 11 comparables have verified PUE | Gravitas 1.08 target is among the most aggressive; industry benchmarks available from Uptime Institute |
| Behind-the-meter structure comparables | Very few public comparables use this model | Crusoe and some Nordic facilities are closest analogues |
| Exact capacity utilization rates | Most operators disclose total/planned but not utilization | Swiss market average >90% occupancy provides a reference |
| Detailed revenue multiples (EV/Revenue) | Requires normalized financial data across private companies | Datenanalyse agent can compute for public comparables (CoreWeave, APLD, Nebius, Northern Data) |
